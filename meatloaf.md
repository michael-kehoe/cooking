# Meatloaf
## Ingrediants
* 1lb Ground beef
* 1lb italian sausage
* 1 onion chooped
* 4 stalks celery/ green onion
* 1/2 Green pepper chopped
* 1 Egg
* 1 cup pork rinds
* Minced garlic to taste
* Salt and pepper to taste
